A Pop-up Zine Shop
At Mule Gallery: 80 Fresno St. in San Francisco
From April 3rd – May 29th 2020

The Ephemeral Bookshop is a pop-up zine shop, gallery show, and event space run by those rascals over at Galaxy Brain. It sells zines and prints from local artists, provides Risograph printing and publishing services, and will continue to change and grow until its inevitable demise.
